Sausage stirfry


Sausage stir fry is another popular dish in Sri Lanka due to its taste and easy preparation. Sausages are quick to cook in any recipe. There is no much preparation other than wash and slice. Therefore I cook this stir fry and some other sausage recipes when I want to prepare a quick meal.

Although this sausage stir fry is a well-known Sri Lankan recipe, I want to share it here in case if someone looks for it.

Ingredients
  • 4-5 Chicken sausages
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2-3 Green chillies
  • 1 sprig of curry leaves
  • 1 inch piece Rampe (Pandan leaf)s
  • ¼ tspn mustard seeds
  • ¼ tspn turmeric powder
  • 1 tspn chili flakesf
  • 1 inch piece cinnamon stick
  • Oil to fry
  • Salt as required
instruction
  1. Wash sausages and cut into bite size pieces.

  2. Keep aside.

  3. Slice onion. Split green chilies lengthwise and keep aside.

  4. In a frying pan, heat about 1-2 tbspn oil.

  5. Add mustard seeds when oil is hot and allow spluttering.

  6. Then add sliced sausages and fry.

  7. Fry few minutes until the colour of sausages turn in to the cooked colour.

  8. Then add sliced onion, curry leaves and green chili. Add rampe piece too.

  9. Mix well and cook for another few seconds.

  10. Finally add chili flakes to the stir-fry and mix well.

  11. A Sri Lankan style sausages stir fry is ready to serve.
